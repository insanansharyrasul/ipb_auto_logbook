IPB Auto Logbook

Extension Companion
A lightweight in-browser automation helper. Load your CSV data and let it fill, attach proof files, and submit logs automatically!
Quick Guide:
  1. Navigate to the IPB Portal page using the button below.
  2. Look for the floating 📄 icon in the bottom-right corner.
  3. Click it to open the control panel, configure Dosen/Row, upload your `data.csv` and files.
  4. Click Start Filling!
